The toolkit

Everything you need to build calm

Each surface is composed from the same translucent material — so depth, focus, and hierarchy emerge naturally instead of being forced.

Layered glass surfaces

Stackable frosted panels with consistent blur, saturation, and border tokens. Depth without heaviness, on any background.

Living aurora engine

Soft drifting color fields that respond to the cursor with gentle parallax — atmosphere that stays subtle, never distracting.

Motion that breathes

Every transition is eased and reduced-motion aware. Hover sheen, 3D tilt, and scroll reveals tuned to feel effortless.

Typographic clarity

Plus Jakarta Sans with airy spacing and a soft light palette — readable over any aurora, in light or dark contexts.

Responsive by design

Fluid type and adaptive grids from 360px to 4K. Glass adapts to context so layouts never feel cramped or empty.

Performance minded

GPU-friendly blur, will-change hints, and rAF-throttled parallax keep things smooth even on modest devices.
